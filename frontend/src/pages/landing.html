The Reality

THE PROBLEM

Millions of rural Indians visit overloaded Outpatient Departments (OPDs) for issues that could be handled remotely.

🚫

No Symptom Assessment

ASHA workers and patients have no way to assess how urgent a symptom is before travelling long distances to a hospital.

🗣️

Language Barrier

Most existing health chatbots are in English only — completely unusable for rural populations who speak Hindi, Gujarati, Marathi, or Tamil.

🏥

Overloaded Hospitals

No tool exists to distinguish between self-care, a clinic visit, or an emergency. Overloaded OPDs can't focus on real emergencies because they handle cases that shouldn't be there.

The result: Preventable deaths from missed emergencies, and avoidable OPD collapse from non-urgent cases with nowhere else to go.

The Solution

WHAT TO BUILD

✅

Multilingual Voice & Text Input

Accepts symptom input in voice or text across regional languages (Hindi, Gujarati, Marathi, Tamil, English).

✅

Rule-Based AI Classification

Uses an advanced health engine to classify urgency into three precise tiers: Self-care, Visit clinic, or Emergency.

✅

Smart Facility Routing

Automatically recommends the nearest appropriate healthcare facility from a database of Primary Health Centres (PHCs) and hospitals.

✅

Zero-Friction Access

Works immediately on any mobile browser or WhatsApp with absolutely no app installation required.
